Beach Bum

The Story

I was challenged to research, design, and create a wave and weather forecasting app for beach lovers around the world.

Problem Statement

"Beach lovers and water sport aficionados need a reliable, quick, and easy way to check beach weather conditions, because they want to plan time at the beach effectively, safely, and have fun."
